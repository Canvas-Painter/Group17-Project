Custom Themes: Create, modify, and manage your own locally stored themes

  • How to Create a Theme:
    1. Select the colors you wish to use by clicking the colored boxes listed
    2. In the "Theme Name" text box, enter a name for your theme (cannot have duplicates)
    3. Finally, click "Save New Theme" and your own personalized theme will update on the page and be saved for re-use
  • How to Update a Theme:
    1. Select the theme you wish to update in the "Current Theme" dropdown
    2. Alter the colors however you wish
    3. Once you're happy with your changes, click "Update Current Theme" and your changes will be saved
  • How to Delete a Theme:
    1. Select the theme you wish to delete in the "Current Theme" dropdown
    2. Click "Delete Current Theme" and the theme will be deleted

Standard Syllabus

For quick access to a course's syllabus:

  1. Navigate to any page within your Canvas course
  2. Look for the "Standard Syllabus" link at the bottom of the sidebar
  3. Click the link to view the course syllabus directly

Note: This link is automatically added to every course page for easy access.

Gradebook Required Grade

The gradebook page will have an additional menu where you can specify a custom assignment

  1. You input the category of the assignment. (If relevant)
  2. You input the points the assignment is worth
  3. You input the total grade in the class you want

And our software will tell you want you would need to get on the assignment to have that grade

Note: This does not take into account what-if grades you put in.

Gamify Canvas Calendar

The Gamify Canvas Calendar integrates fun and motivation into assignment tracking. It is located in the sidebar and includes:

  • A progress bar that visually tracks assignment completion
  • Icons and colored percentages to indicate progress
  • A **point system** where you earn points for completing assignments early
  • Unlockable **visual rewards** based on completion streaks

Note: The Gamify Calendar encourages productivity by rewarding early submissions and tracking progress over time.
